[SSID STRUCTURE]
    ├── Home Area:
    │   ├── SSID: HomeNet (VLAN 10)
    │   └── SSID: GuestWiFi (VLAN 30)
    └── Office Area:
        ├── SSID: HomeNet (VLAN 10)
        ├── SSID: OfficeNet (VLAN 20)
        └── SSID: GuestWiFi (VLAN 30)

[SECURITY & POLICY]
    ├── VLAN 10 (HomeNet): Full Access to all VLANs
    ├── VLAN 20 (OfficeNet): Limited Access to HomeNet + CCTV
    ├── VLAN 30 (Guest): Blocked from Internal VLANs, Internet Only
    ├── VLAN 40 (CCTV): Isolated except access from VLAN 10/20
    └── All VLANs → Internet: Allowed

[MANAGEMENT & MONITORING]
    ├── VLAN 99 (Mgmt) Optional for Router/Switch/AP
    ├── WinBox / WebFig on MikroTik
    ├── DHCP: Centralized or Relay to Reyee
    └── Monitoring: SNMP / Netwatch / Static Reservations

[NOTES]
- MikroTik is the main VLAN Firewall + Inter-router
- Reyee still handles WAN + Upstream NAT
- Guest WiFi → Strict Isolation w/ Client Isolation
- CCTV segmented into VLAN 40, Viewable from Home/Office
- Scalable for future SSID, VLAN, or Fiber uplinks
